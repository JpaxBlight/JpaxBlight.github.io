About Me

Creating expressive characters and stories through bold digital art.

Hi, I’m Jpax Alcomendas — a multidisciplinary artist focused on illustration, character design, comics, digital painting, and stylized visuals. My work blends expressive storytelling, bold shapes, and atmospheric tones to create art that feels alive.

My portfolio showcases a range of styles, from whimsical “kiddie” art to darker, more dramatic compositions. Whatever the medium, my goal is always to craft visuals that resonate, connect, and stay with the viewer.
